About Me


Hello,

My name is Alissa Champigny. I have been in the medical field for 11 years. I graduated high school with a Nursing Assistant License and provided homecare to patients while I attended college. In 2013, I graduated NHTI with an associates degree in Radiologic Technology. Since graduation, I became a nationally registered CT technologist. Currently, I feel I have very limited room for growth in diagnostic imaging.

June 2020 I began UNH's coding bootcamp cohort. I am very excited to return to school and begin a new career in Web Development. Web Development will provide me with the career growth and constant learning that I am looking for.

In my free time, I enjoy spending time with my dog, adventures abroad and locally, and playing in the New England Pinball league. Now my free time is filled with lots of coding and I couldn't be happier.
