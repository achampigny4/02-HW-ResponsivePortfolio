About Me

Hello,

My name is Alissa Champigny. I live in Kingston, New Hampshire. I have been in the medical field for 11 years. I graduated high school with a Nursing Assistant License and while attending college, I provided homecare to patients. In 2013, I graduated NHTI with an associates degree in Radiologic Technology. Since graduation, I became a nationally registered CT technologist.

Currently, I have limited room for growth in diagnostic imaging. In the past I greatly enjoyed using HTML to modify websites. So, in June 2020 I began UNH's coding bootcamp cohort. I am very excited to return to school. I like to continuously learn. A career in Web Development will provide me with the ability to grow professionally which is exactly what I am looking for.

In my free time, I enjoy spending time with my dog, adventures abroad and locally, and playing in the New England Pinball League. Now my free time is filled with a lot of coding and I couldn't be happier.
